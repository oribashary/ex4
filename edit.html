Welcome to the "Model Money City" page!

Here, we've got all the latest info on our models' prices and the cities they're located in. We know you're excited to make an offer, but hold your horses! Before you do that, here's what you need to know:

Offer Outcomes
  • We've got two possible outcomes for your offer: either it'll be accepted, or it'll be denied.
  • If you're lucky enough to have enough money to make an offer, we'll return a message that looks something like this: "model_money_city_accept". That means you're good to go!
  • On the other hand, if you don't have enough cash to make an offer, we'll return a message that looks like this: "model_money_city_denied". Sorry, but we've got to keep our models fed and clothed!

So, go ahead and start browsing our models and their prices, and don't forget to bring your wallet if you're serious about making an offer!
